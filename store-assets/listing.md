**Description:**

Think you know football? Prove it — every single day.

Football Trivia is a daily collection of football puzzle games. Each day brings a fresh set of challenges that ramp up in difficulty through the week — Mondays are friendly, Saturdays are for experts.

THE DAILY LINEUP
• The Grid — one player for every club crossing. Obscure picks score bonus points.
• Missing XI — fill in a famous starting lineup from a real historic match.
• Who Are Ya? — guess the mystery player from clues that narrow with every try.
• Career Path — name the player from their club history. Endless mode, no daily limit.
• Connections — sort 16 players into 4 hidden groups.
• Higher or Lower — call the market value, build the chain.
• Top Lists, Career Timeline, Guess the Match, Blind Ranking, Market Movers, and The Agent — twelve modes in all.

CLIMB THE RANKS
Every correct answer earns XP. Rise from Amateur through Semi-Pro to World Class, keep your daily streak alive, and compare scores on the leaderboard.

FREE TO PLAY
No paywall. Play every mode, every day.

Football Trivia is an unofficial, fan-made quiz game. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any club, league, or federation. All club and competition names are used for identification purposes only.
